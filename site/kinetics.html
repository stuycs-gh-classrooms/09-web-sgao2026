Energy Diagram

y-axis is relative since enthalpy cannot be measured

  • reaction coordinate (x-axis) - percent of reaction done
  • activation energy (E_A) - energy required to start the raction
  • transition state - diagram maximum
  • intermediate - substance in a multi-step reaction that is made and consumed

Rate of Reaction

change in concentration / change in time

  • instantaneous rate - slope of a curve at a point
  • average rate - slope between two points on a curve
  • low constant = reactant favored
  • rate determinating step - slowest step of the reaction; molecularity will determine the order of the full reaction
resource

Molecularity

number of particles involved in the rate determining step

  • first order: one particle in reactants; fixed half-life
  • second order: two particles in reactants; lengthening half-life
  • zero order: shortening half-life
